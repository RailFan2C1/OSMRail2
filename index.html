OSMRail - OpenStreetMap Railsimulator

This is an experimental railsimutaion using OpenStreetMap data.

How to use:

  • select a type of route, enter your city and press search
    • start with small tram or subway routes
  • a list of of routes will apear (or do search for another type)
    • pick one, then check the routeId in a new window/tab
    • roundabouts will cause problems, busses sometimes won't work
  • long routes may slow down performance
    • use erase elements to "clean up" behind you
    • leave away details like trees or buildings
  • press start
    • have patience, loading will take 30 to 60 seconds!
    • use keys to control train
